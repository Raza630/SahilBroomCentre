About Our Company

Our Story

Sahil Broom Centre was founded in 1987 by Mr. Sahil Ahmed with a vision to provide high-quality, affordable Broom to the local community. Starting as a small workshop with just three employees, we have grown into a full-scale manufacturing facility employing over 50 skilled workers.

Our Mission

To produce durable, effective cleaning tools that make everyday cleaning easier and more efficient for households and businesses alike.

Quality Assurance

All our products undergo rigorous quality checks at every stage of production. We use only the finest materials to ensure longevity and performance.
